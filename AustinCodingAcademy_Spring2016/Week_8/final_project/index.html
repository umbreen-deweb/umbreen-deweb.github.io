What I do

As a web developer, I use Front End Development language to create digital websites,
to help you convey your message to the global community.
As an artist, I create abstract art using acrylic as my main medium.
As a realtor I help individuals or families find their perfect home.
I look forward to engaging in each of these creative collaborations to help you best self-express!

"My inspiration is the nature of things, beings and the extraordinary talent that surrounds, humbles and inspires me. Abstract art knows no bounds and I am in love with the surprise that different blends of colors and brush strokes bring about so I can say; "I like how this feels". I never plan anything before I embark on an abstract journey. The path reveals itself as I keep on painting on."
